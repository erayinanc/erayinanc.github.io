About Me

I'm an engineer and scientist with a PhD in computational sciences, currently working at Microsoft Research AI4Science. My primary research focus is on developing AI-driven engineering solutions that leverage computing systems at the exascale.

During my time at the Jülich Supercomputing Centre (JSC), I developed and maintained AI4HPC, an open-source library designed to apply state-of-the-art AI models to complex engineering problems on HPC systems.
