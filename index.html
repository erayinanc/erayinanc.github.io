Eray Inanc’s Website

Eray is an engineer and scientist with PhD in computational sciences working at Microsoft Research AI4Science. His main research interests lie in AI-based engineering solutions exploiting computing systems towards exascale.

Proudly presenting AI4HPC, an open-source library to apply state-of-the-art AI models for complex engineering use-cases on exascale systems developed and maintained by him during his time in Jülich Supercomputing Centre (JSC).
